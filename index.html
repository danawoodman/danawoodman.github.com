Hi, I'm Dana

I am the VP of Web and Mobile at remote.it. We make it secure and easy to remotely access internet connected devices anywhere in the world.

Prior to my role at remote.it, I ran a software consultancy for around 11 years working with a wide range of clients and projects. Projects included mobile applications and web applications for clients like Google, SunEdison, Vendini, Ciena, Swinerton Builders and many others from large to small.

In 2012, I founded the first makerspace in Sonoma County, California, Chimera Arts & Makerspace, which is a space for artists, makers, inventors and businesses to build the things of their dreams. I served as Executive Director from 2012 to 2017 and grew our membership to over 200 paying makers.

I was a co-founder of the disaster information website SonomaFireInfo.com which was created in response to the 2017 fires in Northern California. The website provided up-to-date information provided by our 60+ volunteers and serving over 170,000 unique visitors. It was developed in less than 24 hours and provided helpful resources during the duration of the fires.
